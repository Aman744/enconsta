Director - Engineering

Aravind Srinivasa

LinkedIn Email Website Company Profile

Aravind Srinivasa is a veteran instrumentation and control systems engineer with over 33 years of experience in design, detailed engineering, and supervision of installation and commissioning across onshore and offshore oil & gas plants, renewable energy (wind farm) platforms, HVDC and HVAC platforms, refineries, and petrochemical plants. Currently serving as Deputy Division Head at L & T Chiyoda Ltd in Vadodara, he also holds a concurrent long-standing role as Discipline Manager at Petrofac (since 2001). An Annamalai University alumnus (Bachelor's in Engineering, 1978–1983), Aravind is well-versed in international codes and standards including BP, API, and GOST/SNiP, and is proficient with distributed control systems from Fisher-Rosemount, ABB, Yokogawa, Honeywell, and Siemens. He has led concept, FEED, and detailed engineering activities for instrumentation and telecom disciplines, handled sub-sea and deep-sea project requirements, and managed projects as group head across multiple engineering centers.
