Krishna K.R.S.R is a seasoned EPCM Consultant and MIT Madras alumnus with extensive expertise in engineering, procurement, construction, and project management across the oil & gas, refinery, petrochemical, and power sectors. He is the Founder Member of the "Industry Institute Interface" Forum and Founder of the KPK Foundation. An Honorary Rotarian at the Aditya Chapter of Rotary Club, Chennai, Krishna serves as an Advisory Committee Member at multiple engineering institutions across Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu — including SRM University, Sri Venkateswara University, Sasi Group of Engineering Colleges, Gudlavalleru Engineering College, Akshya Engineering College, SVS Engineering College, and SA Engineering College, among others.
