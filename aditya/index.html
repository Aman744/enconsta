Aditya Krishna is the Founder & Director of Safyrus Media, a Hyderabad-based digital marketing agency that has delivered over 200 successful projects across branding, digital marketing, and software development for clients spanning India, Europe, the Middle East, and the USA. An Ashoka University alumnus, Aditya brings a distinctive blend of engineering and creative leadership — having previously founded and led EPCOGEN Pvt. Ltd., a high-performance engineering services provider for Oil & Gas, Refineries, and Petrochemicals, which he successfully grew and led to acquisition by AXISCADES Technologies Ltd. He has also served as Director of Indian Operations for Digital Trust Networks Inc. (a U.S.-based digital identity firm), Director of Marketing Communications at Vision India Foundation, and Founder of Arkom Engineering Consultants. His career bridges engineering consultancy, digital identity ecosystems, and full-spectrum brand marketing.
